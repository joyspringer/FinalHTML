About Me

I grew up in Oregon and spent a large part of my free time taking photos of the beautiful scenery on the coast. My family and I moved to North Dakota in 2017/2018. Since then, I've pursued my education at MSU and currently live in an apartment in Minot with my two cats: Rajah and Timone. (Pictured Below)

A fundamental belief that I hold that fuels me and my work is the idea that memories are priceless. I strive to create a joyful experience for my clients and help provide tangible memories to cherish forever for them and their families. As far as my past experience in photography - I worked for Lifetouch Portraits in taking church directory photos, have had several photo sessions with friends and family, and have photographed a wedding. Right now my focus is on building my portfolio and my book of business. Please contact me to set up a session!

The photography sessions I offer include all of the following:

  • Family
  • Newborn
  • Senior
  • Wedding
  • Engagement
  • Graduation
  • Maternity

Although I do specialize in portraits like the ones listed above, I also dabble in nature, wildlife, and architectural photography.
